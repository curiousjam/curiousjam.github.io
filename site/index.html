About Essays

I bring emerging AI products to market.

I've spent most of my career in the messy middle where product, customers, partners, trust, GTM, and operations all have to move together.

At Google, I helped build new platform businesses from the ground up. At Meta AI, I worked inside an AI research organization as it scaled. Today, I work with early-stage AI and consumer technology teams turning early customer signal into a repeatable path to market.

I'm especially interested in AI products people have to understand, trust, build on, pay for, create with, or bring into their daily lives.

The question I keep coming back to: how do we build technology that makes people more capable, more creative, more connected, and more human?
